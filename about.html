Sky:COTL is a multiplier game which you can explore different worlds and candle run to find candles which is the games currency.

There are also events that you and possibly your friends can take part of and follow an in-game stories to get limited items or cosmetics which can decorate your avatar in the game.

What are winged lights?

Winged lights are the form of glowing silhouettes. When you've collected enough Winged Light you can level up your Cape, which allows you to fly higher and farther while exploring the game.

How to download Sky Children of the Light

  1. Go to your app store
  2. Type in Sky: Children of the Light and Download
  3. Log in the game and have fun!
